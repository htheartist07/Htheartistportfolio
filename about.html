About me

Who I am?

Welcome to my artistic realm! I am Hermes Andrés Gutiérrez Mesino, an aspiring animator with a passion for bringing stories to life on the silver screen. Within these creative chambers, I invite you to dance between harmony and chaos, exploring the depths of our ever-changing human nature.

This is my journey

My journey as an artist has led me to develop a diverse skill set encompassing creative writing, character design, 2D and 3D animation, and storyboarding. As I navigate through this sea of possibilities, I am driven by a relentless pursuit of beauty in art and a commitment to continuous growth.

What can I give you?

Collaboration lies at the heart of my artistic vision. I thrive in team environments, bringing patience, creativity, and a spirit of innovation to every project. Whether it's crossing seas, scaling mountains, or soaring through skies of imagination, I am always ready to embrace new challenges and contribute my unique perspective.

Now, the world is ours

I invite you to join me on this journey of exploration and creation. Together, let us weave stories that captivate and inspire audiences around the world. If you share my passion for storytelling and visual expression, I would love to connect and explore opportunities for collaboration.

Thank you for stepping into my theatre. Let's create something magical together.
